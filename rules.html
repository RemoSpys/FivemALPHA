Base Building

  • Players may not build inside or within 300m of Monuments, Trevor's Trailer, Drug Zones, Event Zones, Nightmare Zones, Military Bases, Dealerships.
  • Building on roads = NO
  • Players must build at least 200m apart; if deemed too close to another base, you will be warned once, followed by a removal of the base. (This rule applies to Friends, Allies, Friends of Clans / Clan Members)

    • VDM / Vehicle Deathmatch

  • Vehicle deathmatch is identical to random deathmatch, however, the rule explains how vehicles should not be used as weapons. Typically, all forms of VDM are not allowed.
  • You are only allowed to kill someone if you, and the persons are inside of any of the CIRCLE RED ZONES
  • If you are a victim of VDM, you MUST provide a clip during your report. (this is only if logs are unavailable for any reason)

    • Combat Logging

  • Players must not disconnect from a combat situation to avoid negative consequences such as loss of loot, money, or other valuables. If you crash, you must reconnect to the server and inform the other parties via Discord or In-Game Chat.
  • Players caught combat logging will be warned, following harsher punishments such as a kick or ban if further instances of combat logging occur; this is not a rule to take lightly.

    • Clan and Clan Territory Rules

  • Players must not disconnect from a combat situation to avoid negative consequences such as loss of loot, money, or other valuables. If you crash, you must reconnect to the server and inform the other parties via Discord or In-Game Chat.
  • Players caught combat logging will be warned, following harsher punishments such as a kick or ban if further instances of combat logging occur; this is not a rule to take lightly.

    • RDM / Random Deathmatch

  • Vehicle deathmatch is identical to random deathmatch, however, the rule explains how vehicles should not be used as weapons. Typically, all forms of VDM are not allowed.
  • You are only allowed to kill someone if you, and the persons are inside of any of the CIRCLE RED ZONES
  • If you are a victim of VDM, you MUST provide a clip during your report. (this is only if logs are unavailable for any reason)

    • KOS / Kill on Sight

  • Kill on sight (KoS) is the act of killing a player with no engagement.
  • Players are NOT allowed to KoS (Only deemed fit if there are Hackers, Players Mass RDMing).
  • If you are a victim of KoS, you MUST provide a clip during your report. (this is only if logs are unavailable for any reason)
  • Airdrops are considered KoS
  • Airdrops must NOT be dropped onto your base or any player's base, safe zones, quest zones.
  • Airdrops are allowed in Red Zones and ANYWHERE outside of Red Zones. They are KoS.

    • Cheating and Server Exploits

  • Hackers will be permanently banned without warning or chance to appeal.
  • Mods, trainers, and software that enable their user to activate unfair abilities are hacks, this is not debatable.
  • Players must not have the ability to activate cheats on demand while in the server; players must uninstall such mods and trainers before joining the game or risk being automatically detected by our anti-cheat preventions.
  • Players are allowed to use Audio and Visual mods if there are no side effects (These mods cannot improve or change vehicle performance or models or protect the player from gunfire).
  • Players are allowed to bind keys to make it easier to perform actions (usually those that require typing).

    • Robberies and Scamming

  • Players cannot force their victims to withdraw money from an ATM. Under no circumstances can a player force another player to sell or trade assets purchased with USD or via In-Game Credits such as cosmetics, property, houses, vehicles, businesses or other in-game assets. You are only allowed to take In-Game Inventory Items such as Weapons, Consumables, Armors, Masks, and General Loot
  • Players are NOT allowed to take another player's valuables unless they've died in Red Zone marked on the map.
  • Players are NOT allowed to take another player's vehicle. (Secured loot may be inside of someone's car, if you're caught stealing you will be punished accordingly)
  • Players cannot scam during trades; all trades must be completed fairly and both sides must receive the items agreed on.
